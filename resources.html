Resources

Helpful Resources

Here are some organizations and resources that may offer additional support and information:

  • Mental Health America (MHA) - Provides advocacy, education, research, and services.
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) - Dedicated to building better lives for the millions of Americans affected by mental illness.
  • American Association for Marriage and Family Therapy (AAMFT) - Professional organization for MFTs.
  • American Psychological Association (APA) - Resources on various psychological topics.
