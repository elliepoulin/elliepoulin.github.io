About Me

Welcome! I'm Ellie Poulin, a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist dedicated to helping individuals, couples, and families navigate life's challenges and foster healthier relationships. My passion lies in creating a supportive environment where you feel heard, understood, and empowered to make meaningful changes.

I help my clients to identify and understand the current patterns that aren’t working, process past experiences that have shaped current patterns, and learn and practice new thoughts and behaviors for better functioning. By letting go of ineffective patterns, we have access to our authentic selves, we no longer fill a role based on others’ expectations, we are in-tune with what we want, and we feel empowered to meet our own needs. We can finally stop behaviors that aren’t helpful and experience change and better functioning.
