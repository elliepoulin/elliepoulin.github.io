Mental Health Organizations & Associations

These reputable organizations offer valuable information, support, and advocacy for mental health and well-being.

National Organizations

  • Mental Health America (MHA) - Provides advocacy, education, research, and services.
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) - Dedicated to building better lives for the millions of Americans affected by mental illness.
  • American Association for Marriage and Family Therapy (AAMFT) - Professional organization for MFTs.
  • American Psychological Association (APA) - Resources on various psychological topics.
  • SAMHSA (Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration) - Offers information and resources for mental health and substance use disorders.

California Organizations

  • California Association of Marriage and Family Therapists (CAMFT) - The largest professional organization for LMFTs in California.
  • Mental Health Services Oversight and Accountability Commission (MHSOAC) - California - Oversees the implementation of the Mental Health Services Act.
  • California Department of Health Care Services (DHCS) - Mental Health - Provides information on mental health services in California.
  • NAMI California - The state organization of NAMI, offering local support and advocacy.
