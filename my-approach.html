My Approach to Therapy

My therapeutic approach is integrative, drawing from various modalities to tailor sessions to your unique needs. I believe in a collaborative process, where we work together to identify your goals and build upon your existing strengths. Therapy is a journey of self-discovery and growth, and I am here to guide and support you every step of the way.

Client-Centered Care

At the heart of my practice is a client-centered philosophy. This means that you are the expert on your own life, and my role is to facilitate your self-discovery and provide tools for growth. I offer a non-judgmental, empathetic, and confidential space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings openly without fear of criticism. Your voice and your experience are paramount in our sessions.

Evidence-Based Practices

I incorporate a variety of evidence-based practices, adapting them to best serve your individual or relational needs. Some of the core modalities I draw from include:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Helps identify and challenge negative thought patterns that contribute to emotional distress and unhelpful behaviors.
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) Informed Strategies: Focuses on developing skills in mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness.
  • Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) Principles: Particularly useful in couples and family therapy, this approach helps identify and transform negative emotional cycles and create secure attachments.
  • Family Systems Theory: Understands individuals within the context of their family and social systems, recognizing how these interactions influence behavior and well-being.

Holistic Perspective

I consider the whole person – mind, body, and spirit – and how various aspects of your life interact to influence your well-being. This includes looking at your relationships, lifestyle, values, and cultural background. My aim is to help you develop a comprehensive understanding of yourself and cultivate lasting positive change that extends beyond our sessions into all areas of your life.

My commitment is to provide compassionate, ethical, and effective therapy that empowers you to build resilience, enhance relationships, and lead a more fulfilling life.
