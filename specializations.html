Areas of Specialization & Common Issues

Ellie Poulin, LMFT offers compassionate and effective therapy for a wide range of concerns. Below are some of the areas where clients often seek support:

Individual Concerns

  • Narcissistic Abuse
  • Complex Trauma (C-PTSD)
  • Neurodiversity (ASD and ADHD)
  • Social Anxiety
  • Self-Esteem issues
  • Depression and Suicidality
  • Anxiety (General Anxiety, Panic Attacks, etc.)
  • Codependency
  • Grief and Loss
  • Life Stages and Changes (e.g., Midlife Crisis, Quarter-Life Crisis)
  • Chronic Mental Health Issues

Relationship & Family Dynamics

  • Relationship Issues (Communication, Conflict, Intimacy)
  • Marital Issues
  • Parenting Issues
  • Separation/Divorce
  • Postpartum Anxiety/Depression (related to family adjustment)
  • Immigrant Families and Bicultural Individuals
  • Military/Veteran Issues (and their families)

If you don't see your specific concern listed, please don't hesitate to reach out. We can discuss your unique situation and determine if my services are the right fit for you.
